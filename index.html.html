ABOUT THE FOUNDER


Barrister Abimbola Charles Olajuyin;
Born on the 14th of December 1963, this exceptional legal practitioner has garnered accolades for his exceptional contributions to the field of law and solvency practice. He has demonstrated exceptional intellectual acumen and passion for justice from an early age. Driven by a desire to bring about positive change in the lives of individuals and businesses, he embarked on a path to study law, enrolling in Bendel State University (Ambrose Alli University) where he bagged an LL.b(Hons).

The rigorous academic journey shaped his legal acumen, honing their advocacy skills, and instilling in them a strong commitment serving of the society. After successfully completing his legal studies in Nigeria Law in Lagos State, and being called to Bar, the founder cut his horn by working at the Special Duties Depart of the Millitary Administrator's office in Uyo, Akwa Ibom state where he gained valuable experience in mediation between oil Producing and Services companies and their host Communities. Thereafter he moved on to gather further experience by working with reputable law firms in Port Harcourt and Abuja, Nigeria. the founder gained valuable experience by working with reputable law firms and financial institutions in Nigeria.
